Diagram Figure Pr.32 - Product Price
When an enterprise agrees to deliver a product to a customer, a price is associated with the Product that has to be recorded. The figure below presents the set of entities that are used to hold this information. The ProductPrice represents an amount, usually of money, that represents the actual price paid by a customer for a purchase, a rent or a lease of a Product. The ProductPrice is associated with the Product entity and the ProductOfferingPrice entity. The structure of ProductPrice subclasses resembles the structure of ProductOfferingPrice subclasses.
